At Above Grind, we understand that sometimes returns are necessary. Rest assured, we make the return process simple and hassle-free, offering swift and courteous assistance to ensure your complete satisfaction with our products. Products can be returned once they have not been opened or used.

Return Process

    Initiate Return:

  • Contact our customer service team via email or phone to initiate the return process. Provide your order details and reason for the return.

    • Receive Authorization:

  • Once your return request is approved, you will receive a return authorization code along with instructions on how to proceed. Package Items: Securely package the items you wish to return, ensuring they are in their original condition and packaging.

    • Shipping:

  • Affix the return authorization code on the package and ship it back to us using a trackable shipping method of your choice. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part.

    • Processing:

  • Once we receive the returned items, our team will inspect them to ensure they meet our return policy criteria.

    • Refund or Exchange:

  • Upon successful inspection, we will process your refund or exchange as per your request and notify you accordingly.

    • We strive to make the returns process as straightforward and convenient as possible, ensuring that our valued customers are satisfied with their Above Grind experience.
